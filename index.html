Welcome to Drama Class!

As one of the 5 elective classes to choose from here in Springfield PB3, Drama is not only where Springfield students learn to act, narrate, make scripts and sharpen their skills as performers and as backstage crew members, it's also where we grow and to strengthen the theoretical & historical knowledge of students about the world of Drama, and it's also where we teach the students of Drama to work together and coordinate with each other to display and craft the best performance possible.

What Students Learn in Drama

The Art of Acting

In Drama, Students will be taught and learn how to act, express, imitate personalities and to enact scences, events and stories. The very basics of performing arts.

The Power of Planning & Practice

Students will understand and acknowledge the importance of practice and planning, as both are very crucial for making a good play. 

The Helpfulness of Teamwork 

Students will also understand and acknowledge the importance of teamwork and will also learn to master teamworking and coordination

Learning Scriptwriting

In a play, the script is as important as the performance, the props and the costumes and students are expected to learn its importance and its arts.

The Theory and History of Drama

Students will not only learn the practical skills of performing arts in the form of acting, scripwriting and planning, but also the history behind drama and additional theoretical knowledge that will boost their skills & abilities in Drama.

Using Props & Costumes

Students will also learn that in the process of planning, the preparation of props & costumes is also important for a good play. And so is how they will use those props well in a performance.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Mission is to help students master the necessary practical skills and learn important theoretical knowledge about performing arts to help them become wonderful, talented performers.

Our Vision is to make students learn life skills and useful knowledge from the process of making a performance
