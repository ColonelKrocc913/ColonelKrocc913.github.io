Welcome to Drama Class!

As one of the 5 elective classes to choose from here in Springfield PB3, Drama is not only where students of Springfield learn to act, narrate, or script and sharpen their skills as people who play roles in a Drama show, but it's also where we help students to grow and to strengthen their theoretical & historical knowledge of the world of Drama and to learn to work and coordinate together to make the best play possible.
